About UVA CS Connect

UVA CS Connect is a platform built by UVA students to help peers collaborate, network, and stay up-to-date on computer science events and opportunities across the university.

Our Team

UVA CS Connect was created by a team of four dedicated UVA students:

  • Connor Brooks
  • Nishana Dahal
  • Eva Butler
  • Madeline Wise

Our mission is to provide UVA students with an easy-to-use hub for connecting with peers, learning about events, and sharing opportunities within the CS community.
