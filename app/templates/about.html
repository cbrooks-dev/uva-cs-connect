About UVA CS Connect

UVA CS Connect began as a student-led initiative to strengthen the culture of collaboration within the Computer Science community at the University of Virginia. Our goal is to make connection and opportunity as integral to UVA CS as the classroom experience itself.

Our Story

What started as a small peer-networking group has evolved into a vibrant platform connecting hundreds of UVA CS students across disciplines and career interests. From study groups and technical workshops to mentorship and project teams, CS Connect empowers students to learn collaboratively and apply their skills beyond the classroom.

Our Vision

We believe that real learning happens in community. UVA CS Connect envisions a department where collaboration is second nature, where students have access to peers, mentors, and resources that inspire confidence and growth.
